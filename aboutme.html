why make this?

i always wanted a place to centralize all my art since it tends to be pretty scattered around the place. i also i find social media a bit too limiting in presentation so i decided to make a site to host my art as well as some personal elements like a blog.

about me

hi! i'm harrison. i'm an artist who primarily explores infinity vs intimacy (e.g. space vs earth, abstraction vs concrete, others vs self). i'm primarily a musician, but i also like to dabble in film, photography, writing, and hopefully eventually game development.

why make art?

i mainly make art for two reasons:

one: it is a form a expression, not necessarily self-expression as i'm just an expression of the things that have occured in order for me to exist. but more in the form of needing to express ideas/feelings that i don't see elsewhere in the world.

two: art is one of the few gods that you can pray to that will leave you fulfilled no matter the result. its all about the journey and experiencing things as they are which i think is very intrinsic to the human experience. it also makes you confront yourself and the world around you in a way no other passion does, but im just 20 so who knows lol. at least it beats climbing a corporate ladder.
